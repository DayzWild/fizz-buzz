The FizzBuzz-Challenge

Fizz Buzz is a well-known children's learning game in which the players usually sit in a circle and count out loud from one upwards. All numbers divisible by three must be replaced by the word Fizz and all numbers divisible by five must be replaced by the word Buzz. If a number is divisible by three and five, then Fizz Buzz is called out.
